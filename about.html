About

A place for the children of the men of the Regiments to gather to honor and share memories of their Fathers with each other. The only way we will be able to keep the Regiments' legacy alive is to be able to pass on the stories of the men who served to the children who will follow us.

About the Organization

Descendants of the USA Filipino Regiments 1942â€“1946 Inc. is a Texas-based nonprofit organization established in April 2024. Recognized as a 501(c)(3) public charity, its mission centers on honoring and preserving the legacy of Filipino American soldiers who served in U.S. military units during World War II.

Mission and Activities

  • Historical Preservation: Documenting and sharing the stories of Filipino American veterans from units such as the 1st and 2nd Filipino Infantry Regiments.
  • Community Engagement: Organizing events and initiatives that educate the public about the contributions of these veterans.
  • Advocacy: Supporting efforts to recognize and honor the service of Filipino American WWII veterans, including campaigns for awards like the Congressional Gold Medal.
