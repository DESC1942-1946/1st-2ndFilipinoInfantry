Welcome

Welcome to the official site honoring the 1st and 2nd Filipino Infantry Regiments. We are dedicated to preserving the memory and legacy of our fathers and relatives who served with honor and courage. Please explore, share stories, and help us keep this history alive for future generations.

About

A place for the children of the men of the Regiments to gather to honor and share memories of their Fathers with each other. The only way we will be able to keep the Regiments' legacy alive is to be able to pass on the stories of the men who served to the children who will follow us.
